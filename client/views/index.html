ThinkerToy Playground

Welcome to the ThinkerToy Playground!
ThinkerToys are creative thinking techniques that challenges your brain to think outside of your normal perspectives in order to find new ideas that may provide solutions to your existing problems. Or if you're like me, to find existing problems and then look for solutions!

These tools for inspiring fresh outlooks are easy to use and fun to apply to any challenging aspect of our daily lives whether in school, work, or play. The key is to understand our challenge first - to state clearly exactly what is our goal, and then to write it down so we don't forget! That is what the text box is for, to record any and every flow of thought that enters our head as we are playing with a ThinkerToy.

Notice I emphasized playing. This is a ThinkerToy playground and we come here to have fun with a productive mindset. Creativity can only spark within us when we're in flow with our environment and our invironment.. So drop all your worries, click up a toy, and enjoy!
